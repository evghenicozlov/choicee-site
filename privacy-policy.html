Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Choicee (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

By using the app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require account creation or direct personal information to use the app.

We may collect or process the following data:

1.1 Automatically collected data

• Device information (model, OS version, language)
• App usage data (screens viewed, interactions)
• Approximate location (country/region)
• Advertising identifiers (such as Apple IDFA or Google Advertising ID)

1.2 Advertising data We use third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdMob) which may collect:

• Advertising ID
• Device identifiers
• IP address
• Approximate location
• Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

2. Advertising

The app displays ads using third-party services, including:

• App Open ads
• Banner ads
• Interstitial ads (full-screen ads)

These ad formats may be shown at different times during app usage.

Advertising partners may use cookies, SDKs, and similar technologies to:

• Serve ads (personalized or non-personalized)
• Measure ad performance
• Prevent fraud and abuse

3. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On Apple devices, we request user permission via App Tracking Transparency (ATT) where required.

If you allow tracking, your device identifier may be used to deliver personalized ads. If you do not allow tracking, you will still see ads, but they may be non-personalized.

4. Consent (EEA / UK / other regions)

If you are located in regions that require consent (such as the EEA or UK):

• We request your consent before showing personalized advertising
• You can accept or refuse personalized ads
• Refusing consent will not affect core functionality of the app

You can also change your advertising consent at any time inside the app: Settings → Ads Consent

5. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

• Operate and improve the app
• Display advertisements
• Measure performance and stability
• Analyze usage trends

6. Your Privacy Choices

ou can control advertising settings:

iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking

Android: Settings → Google → Ads

You may also reset or limit your advertising ID at any time.

7. Third-Party Services

We use third-party providers that may process data according to their own privacy policies, including:

Google AdMob
Apple services (ATT framework)

We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

8. Data Retention

We do not store personal user data on our own servers. Data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective policies.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no system is 100% secure.

10. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under the age of 12.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 12.

11. Age Recommendation

Due to the presence of advertising (including interstitial and app open ads), the app is generally recommended for users aged 12+.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app or on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
