Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Choicee mobile application (hereby referred to as the "Application"), created by the Service Provider. The Application is provided as a free service and is intended for use "AS IS".

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a transparent approach to how information is handled.

Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed to function without collecting any personal or non-personal data.

We do not collect, store, or process any of the following:

Personal information (such as name, email address, or contact details)
Device identifiers or IP address
Usage data (such as interactions, session duration, or feature usage)
Location data (precise or approximate)
Any behavioral or analytics data

All features of the Application are fully functional without requiring any data collection.

Data Processing Principles

We follow key privacy principles, including:

Data minimization — we do not collect data that is not strictly necessary
Privacy by design — the Application is built to avoid data collection entirely
User control — all interactions remain local to your device

Third-Party Services

The Application does not integrate with third-party services that collect, process, or transmit user data.

We do not use:

Analytics tools
Advertising networks
Tracking technologies
External data processors

Data Storage

All application settings and preferences are stored locally on your device only.

We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to external servers.

International Data Transfers

Since no data is collected or transmitted, no cross-border data transfers occur.

Opt-Out Rights

Because the Application does not collect any data, there is no data processing to opt out from.

You can stop using the Application at any time by uninstalling it through standard device procedures.

Data Retention

We do not retain any personal or non-personal data.

Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed to children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any users.

Security

Since the Application does not collect or transmit data, the risk associated with data breaches is minimized.

We still aim to follow industry best practices to ensure the Application operates securely.

Legal Compliance

We aim to comply with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), by ensuring that no personal data is collected or processed.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in legal, technical, or business developments.

Any updates will be posted within the Application or on this page.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy.
